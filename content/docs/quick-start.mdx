Get up and running with Tyform in less than 5 minutes.

## Step 1: Create an Account

<Steps>
  <Step>
    Go to [tyform.com/signup](/signup)
  </Step>
  <Step>
    Sign up with your email or Google account
  </Step>
  <Step>
    Verify your email address
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Step 2: Create Your First Form

<Steps>
  <Step>
    Click **"Create Form"** in your dashboard
  </Step>
  <Step>
    Choose a blank form or start from a template
  </Step>
  <Step>
    Give your form a title
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Step 3: Add Questions

Click **"Add Question"** to add different question types:

- **Short Text** — Single line answers
- **Long Text** — Multi-line paragraph responses
- **Email** — Email validation included
- **Multiple Choice** — Select one option
- **Checkbox** — Select multiple options
- **Rating** — Star or number ratings
- **Date** — Date picker
- **File Upload** — Accept file uploads (Pro)

## Step 4: Customize Your Form

### Theme Settings
- Choose colors that match your brand
- Select fonts for headings and body text
- Add a custom background

### Form Settings
- Enable/disable progress bar
- Set form start and end screens
- Configure submission limits

## Step 5: Share Your Form

Once your form is ready:

<Steps>
  <Step>
    Click **"Publish"** to make it live
  </Step>
  <Step>
    Copy the shareable link
  </Step>
  <Step>
    Share via email, social media, or embed on your website
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Embedding Options

```html
<!-- Embed as iframe -->
<iframe 
  src="https://tyform.com/f/your-form-id" 
  width="100%" 
  height="600"
  frameborder="0"
></iframe>
```

## Step 6: View Responses

Navigate to your form's **Responses** tab to:

- View individual responses
- See summary charts and analytics
- Export responses to CSV
- Set up email notifications

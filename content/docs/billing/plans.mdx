## Free Plan

**Includes:**

- 3 forms
- 100 responses per month
- 10 questions per form
- Basic question types
- Basic logic
- Email notifications
- Standard support

**Limitations:**

- Tyform branding on forms
- 30-day response retention
- No file uploads
- No API access

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Can I change plans anytime?

Yes! Upgrade or downgrade at any time. Changes take effect immediately.

- **Upgrading:** Prorated charge for remaining period
- **Downgrading:** Credit applied to next billing cycle

### What happens if I exceed my response limit?

- **Free:** Your form stops accepting responses
- **Pro:** Additional responses charged at $0.02 each
- **Business:** No limit

### Do you offer refunds?

We offer a 14-day money-back guarantee on all paid plans. No questions asked.

### Is there a discount for nonprofits?

Yes! Nonprofits and educational institutions get 50% off. [Apply here](/nonprofit)

### Can I pay annually?

Yes! Annual billing saves you 25% compared to monthly billing.

| Plan | Monthly | Annual (per month) | Savings |
|------|---------|-------------------|---------|
| Pro | $39 | $29 | $120/year |
| Business | $129 | $99 | $360/year |

### What payment methods do you accept?

- Credit/debit cards (Visa, Mastercard, Amex)
- PayPal
- Bank transfer (Business plan, annual only)
- Invoice (Enterprise)
